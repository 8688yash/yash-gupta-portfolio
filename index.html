About Me

My Journey: From Sea to Silicon

I am Yash Gupta. My journey began as a Merchant Navy Cadet, having completed my BSc Nautical Science from TS Chanakya (Indian Maritime University, Navi Mumbai Campus). This foundational maritime experience instilled discipline, resilience, and a deep understanding of the global shipping industry. This foundation fueled my passion for innovation, leading me to transition into the exciting realms of technology and Artificial Intelligence.

I am a published author, a digital innovator, and the founder of Digital Harbour and Naavik. My commitment to education is profound, having had the privilege to mentor over 10,000 students across 100+ schools globally, sharing insights into technology and the future of maritime.

My vision is clear: to digitize and innovate the maritime sector using cutting-edge technologies like AI, blockchain, and advanced analytics. My mission is to build impactful, accessible tech solutions that truly serve and uplift the global seafaring community, making their lives safer, more efficient, and more connected.

SeaMate AI - The Siri of the Sea

SeaMate AI is an AI-powered smart assistant app crafted specifically for seafarers. Its core purpose is to revolutionize life at sea by improving safety, productivity, and communication.

Key Features:

  • Voice-based and chatbot interaction for intuitive use.
  • Task scheduler with reminders for daily shipboard duties.
  • AI-powered safety compliance checks and alerts.
  • Crew wellness tracker with multilingual support.
  • Offline mode for disconnected environments, ensuring continuous operation.
  • Emergency reporting and comprehensive training module.

Tech Stack:

Flutter (cross-platform app), TensorFlow Lite (AI for on-device processing), Firebase (backend services), Python (backend logic), SQLite (offline mode).

Status: MVP under testing.

Vision: To be the Siri of the Sea — a digital companion improving every aspect of a seafarer's daily work and safety.

BlueCarbon MVP - Blockchain for Maritime Sustainability

BlueCarbon MVP is a blockchain-based carbon credit platform designed to drive maritime sustainability through transparent and verifiable carbon credit operations.

Key Features:

  • Vessel onboarding and registration for emissions tracking.
  • Sensor simulation for realistic data generation (roadmap to real sensors).
  • Carbon project submissions and verification processes.
  • Secure and transparent credit trading marketplace.

Tech Stack:

React (frontend), Firebase (backend & database), Simulated Smart Contracts (current, roadmap to full Web3 blockchain integration).

Status: MVP under development.

Vision: To enable verifiable and transparent carbon credit operations in the maritime industry, incentivizing sustainable practices globally.

Naavik: Edtech Platform for Maritime & Vocational Training

Naavik is an edtech platform primarily focused on career counseling, vocational training, and specialized education, with a significant emphasis on the Merchant Navy sector. Our mission is to empower students and individuals by equipping them with industry-oriented skills and awareness to foster successful careers. Naavik is recognized as an "edtech platform providing industry oriented marketing training & internship programs and provides career counselling awareness seminars and webinars on merchant navy."

Our Core Programs & Offerings:

Merchant Navy Career Counselling & Awareness

Educating senior secondary students (grades 9-12) about career pathways within the Merchant Navy through structured sessions and webinars. Proactive early career guidance for formative development.

Marketing Training & Internship Program

Comprehensive online training by industrial experts, practical experience via live and capstone projects, and valuable letters of recommendation. Aimed at framing "PI-SHAPED PROFESSIONALS" with dual certification.

Vocational Courses

Detailed courses in Office Management, Shorthand/Stenography, and Typing. Focuses on skill development, workplace preparation, and enhancing job opportunities for career improvement.

Inclusive Special Education Services

Niche offering including IMUCET preparation and GP Rating Familiarisation Courses. Maritime professionals develop individualized education plans, providing tailored support within the maritime domain.

Naavik emphasizes practical, industry-oriented skills across all programs, recognizing the gap between traditional education and modern job market demands. This focus ensures graduates are immediately employable and competitive.

Website & User Experience:

The Naavik website features a clear, straightforward structure with a prominent navigation bar. Content is organized into distinct, visually categorized sections, enhancing navigability and information retrieval. The design prioritizes readability and functionality, utilizing a professional blue and white color scheme for a trustworthy impression.
