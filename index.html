About Me

My Journey: From Sea to Silicon

I am Yash Gupta, a passionate visionary at the intersection of maritime innovation and advanced technology. My journey began as a dedicated Merchant Navy Cadet, where I gained invaluable first-hand experience and a profound understanding of the global shipping industry. I proudly completed my BSc Nautical Science from TS Chanakya (Indian Maritime University, Navi Mumbai Campus), equipping me with a strong operational foundation.

This rich maritime background fueled my drive to innovate, propelling me into the dynamic realms of Artificial Intelligence and cutting-edge technology. Beyond my technical pursuits, I am a published author, a recognized digital innovator, and the proud founder of Digital Harbour, a platform dedicated to maritime and tech education, and Naavik, a community and support hub for seafarers.

My commitment to education and mentorship is deep-rooted; I've had the immense privilege to directly mentor over 10,000 students across 100+ schools globally, sharing insights into the transformative power of technology in the maritime sector. My overarching vision is clear: to digitize, automate, and innovate the maritime industry using advanced technologies like AI, blockchain, and robust data analytics. My mission is to build impactful, accessible, and scalable tech solutions that genuinely uplift the global seafaring community, making their lives safer, more efficient, and more connected.

SeaMate AI - The Siri of the Sea

SeaMate AI is an innovative, AI-powered smart assistant app meticulously crafted to serve the unique needs of seafarers. Its core purpose is to revolutionize life at sea by significantly enhancing safety protocols, boosting on-board productivity, and streamlining communication channels for a more connected and efficient maritime environment.

Key Features:

  • Intuitive Interaction: Seamless voice-based commands and a responsive chatbot interface for natural user engagement.
  • Dynamic Task Management: An advanced task scheduler complete with smart reminders for daily shipboard duties, ensuring no critical operation is missed.
  • Proactive Safety Compliance: AI-powered checks that monitor safety regulations and provide real-time alerts for potential non-compliance, enhancing on-board safety.
  • Holistic Crew Wellness: A comprehensive crew wellness tracker, actively promoting mental and physical well-being, with robust multilingual support for a diverse crew.
  • Uninterrupted Operation: A critical offline mode ensuring full functionality and data access even in disconnected or remote maritime environments.
  • Emergency Response & Training: Streamlined emergency reporting features and an extensive training module to prepare seafarers for various scenarios.

Tech Stack:

Frontend: Flutter (for a beautiful, cross-platform mobile application).
AI/ML: TensorFlow Lite (enabling efficient on-device AI processing for speed and offline capability).
Backend & Database: Firebase (scalable backend services for data storage, authentication, and real-time updates).
Core Logic: Python (for robust backend logic and AI model development).
Offline Storage: SQLite (for reliable local data storage when internet connectivity is limited).

Status: MVP under rigorous testing and refinement, with ongoing user feedback integration.

Vision: Our ultimate goal is for SeaMate AI to evolve into the definitive "Siri of the Sea" — a truly indispensable digital companion that intuitively improves every facet of a seafarer's daily work life and significantly enhances safety standards across the global maritime industry.

BlueCarbon MVP - Blockchain for Maritime Sustainability

BlueCarbon MVP is a pioneering blockchain-based carbon credit platform meticulously designed to drive genuine maritime sustainability. Our objective is to establish a verifiable and transparent ecosystem for carbon credit operations within the shipping industry, fostering a new era of environmental accountability.

Key Features:

  • Seamless Vessel Onboarding: A streamlined process for registering vessels and integrating their emissions tracking data onto the platform.
  • Robust Sensor Simulation: Advanced simulation capabilities for generating realistic data, laying the groundwork for future integration with actual on-vessel sensors.
  • Transparent Carbon Project Submissions: A clear and secure mechanism for submitting and verifying carbon reduction projects.
  • Secure Credit Trading Marketplace: A reliable and transparent marketplace for the issuance, trading, and retirement of verifiable carbon credits.

Tech Stack:

Frontend: React (for a dynamic and intuitive user interface).
Backend & Database: Firebase (providing scalable backend services for data management and user authentication).
Blockchain Logic: Simulated Smart Contracts (current implementation, with a clear roadmap towards full Web3 blockchain integration for decentralized operations).

Status: MVP currently under active development, focusing on core functionality and user experience.

Vision: Our ambition is to become the leading platform enabling truly verifiable and transparent carbon credit operations within the maritime industry, creating powerful incentives for global sustainable shipping practices.

Naavik: Pioneering Edtech for Maritime & Vocational Excellence

Naavik operates as a dynamic edtech platform primarily focused on comprehensive career counseling, vocational training, and specialized education, with a significant emphasis on empowering individuals for successful careers in the Merchant Navy sector and beyond. Our digital presence serves as the central hub for disseminating information about our diverse programs and engaging with prospective students.

Our Core Programs & Offerings:

Merchant Navy Career Counselling & Awareness

Specifically designed to educate senior secondary students (grades 9-12) about viable career pathways within the Merchant Navy, delivered through structured interactive sessions and informative webinars. This offers proactive guidance at a formative stage.

Marketing Training & Internship Program

A comprehensive program offering online training led by distinguished industrial experts, providing invaluable practical experience through live and capstone projects directly with NAAVIK. Participants earn dual certification (completion and internship) and valuable letters of recommendation. We explicitly aim at framing "PI-SHAPED PROFESSIONALS".

Vocational Courses

Provides detailed, practical courses in Office Management, Shorthand/Stenography, and Typing. Our focus is on tangible skill development, workplace preparation, increasing job opportunities, career improvement, CV enhancement, and essential soft skill development.

Inclusive Special Education Services

A highly specialized offering that includes intensive IMUCET preparation batches and practical GP Rating Familiarisation Courses. A unique aspect is the direct involvement of experienced maritime professionals who work with students to develop individualized education plans, ensuring tailored support within the maritime domain.

Naavik consistently emphasizes practical, industry-oriented skills across all its programs. This robust focus recognizes the existing gap between traditional academic education and the dynamic demands of the modern job market, ensuring our graduates are immediately employable and highly competitive.
